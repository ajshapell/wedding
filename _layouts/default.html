Our Story

In the early days of 2013, after being introduced by a mutual friend, Beth and Ari headed to Hell's Kitchen for their first date. Beth suggested a bar that gave away free hot dogs. After Ari watched Beth take down 7 hot dogs, he knew it was love.

A few months later, Beth was finally ready to hear Ari say the L word. One morning, Beth could feel it coming, this was the moment. Ari proclaimed: I love Yoo...... Hoo. United by their joint love for YooHoo, they knew it was meant to be.

Fast forward 4 years and 1 dog - Ari took Beth and Rosie away for the weekend to one of their favorite NYC getaways, New Paltz. Right before dinner with Rosie as his witness, Ari got down on one knee and proposed! The next morning to Beth's surprise, the siblings appeared to share in the celebration.

It's been an amazing 4 years and we can't wait to get married surrounded by all the people we love! Thank you for all your support, friendship, and love. Is there any better way to ring in 2018!?
